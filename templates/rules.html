Bus Pass Rules & Regulations

1. Eligibility & Application

Bus passes are issued for Hostellers and Day Scholars:

  • Hostellers bus passes can be scanned only once on their intended reach date provided during booking.
  • Day Scholars bus passes are issued on a 3-month or 6-month basis, depending on their advance payment.
  • During the valid period, the pass can be scanned any number of times for verification.

2. Bus Pass Usage

  • The bus pass is non-transferable. Lending it to others is strictly prohibited.
  • Day Scholars must carry a valid bus pass daily, while Hostellers must carry it only on their intended reach date.

3. QR Code Verification

  • Security personnel will scan the QR code when students exit the bus at the college stop.
  • The system will check the validity of the pass and its expiry date.
  • If a pass is found invalid or expired, the student will either have to pay a â‚¹300 fine to the office or face account suspension, depending on the severity of the case.

4. General Conduct

  • Students must behave respectfully with the driver, staff, and fellow passengers.
  • Misconduct, including harassment, vandalism, or violence, will result in strict disciplinary action.
  • Day Scholars must enter from their designated stop and get off at their designated drop-off location.
  • All students must exit the bus only at the college stop in the morning.

5. Emergency & Contact

For any issues related to the bus pass, contact the Moderator.
